RailTwin
Smart Railway Management powered by Digital Twin, AI, and IoT
RailTwin is a smart railway management platform that combines Digital Twin technology, artificial intelligence, and IoT to deliver real-time monitoring, predictive maintenance, asset tracking, and operational analytics — helping railway operators improve safety, efficiency, and decision-making.
Features
Real-Time Monitoring — Live visibility into track, train, and infrastructure status through a digital twin of the railway network.
Predictive Maintenance — AI-driven analysis of sensor data to detect anomalies and predict failures before they happen.
Asset Tracking — End-to-end tracking of rolling stock, signaling equipment, and infrastructure assets.
Operational Analytics — Dashboards and reports that turn operational data into actionable insights.
How It Works
RailTwin ingests data from IoT sensors deployed across the rail network (track, trains, signals, stations) and feeds it into a continuously updated digital twin. AI models analyze this data stream to surface maintenance needs, track asset health, and generate analytics that support operational decisions.
